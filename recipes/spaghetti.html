Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is a lovely Spaghetti with Meat Sauce recipe that tastes like it was ripped straight from the pages of Italy itself. You'll savor every bite of this amazing dish. And your family will be saying "Mama Mia!" in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1Lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion (Chopped)
  • 4 Cloves Garlic (Minced)
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper (Diced)
  • 1 28oz Can of Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 16oz Can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 6oz Can of Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. 1) Combine Ground Beef, Onion, Garlic, and Green Pepper on the Stove over Medium-High heat. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumly and vegetables are tender. 5-7 Minutes. Drain Grease.
  2. 2) Stir Diced Tomatoes, Tomato Sauce, and Tomato Paste into the Beef and Veg mixture. Season with Oregano, Basil, Salt, and Pepper.
  3. 3) Simmer for 1 Hour. Stir occasionally
  4. 4) With 30 minutes remaning on the sauce, begin boiling water in a pot for the Spaghetti Noodles.
  5. 5) When water begins to boil, add desired amount of Pasta. Bring to simmer, then reduce heat. Cook until Pasta is tender. 5-10 Minutes.
  6. 6) Drain pasta and return to pot.
  7. 7) Pour the finished sauce into the pasta and stir until combined completely.
  8. 8) Serve hot and enjoy!
