Lasagna Recipe

Description

You, Your Family, and Your Neighbors will LOVE this Lasagna recipe! It's full of creamy and melted cheese, a savory meat sauce, and perfectly done Lasagna Noodles. You can freeze it after you finish to eat later. Or serve hot and refrigerate the leftovers. It microwaves to pefection!

Ingredients

  • 1Lbs Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4Lbs Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Cup Onion (Minced)
  • 2 Cloves Garlic (Crushed)
  • 1 28oz Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 6.5oz Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 6oz Cans of Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2 Tbsps White Sugar
  • 4 Tbsps Chopped Fresh Parsely (Divided)
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Dried Basil Leaves
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Salt (Divided) or to taste
  • 1 Tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Tsp Fennel Seeds
  • 1/4 Tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 16oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4Lbs Mozzarella Cheese (Sliced)
  • 3/4 Cup Paresan Cheese (Grated)

Steps

  1. 1) Cook Sausage, Ground Beef, Onion, and Garlic in a Dutch oven over Medium heat until well browned.
  2. 2) Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Seasonw ith Sugar, 2 Tbsps parsley, basil, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper.
  3. 3) Cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours. Stirring occasionally
  4. 4) Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes.
  5. 5) Drain noodles and rinse with cold water
  6. 6) In a mixing bowl combine Ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 Tbsps parsley, and 1/2 tsp of salt.
  7. 7) Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees F (190 Degrees C)
  8. 8) To assemble spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in bottom of a 9x12 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce overlapping slightly. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3rd of the Mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over Mozzarella and sprinle with 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.
  9. 9) Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Cover with foil. To prevent sticking spray the foil with Cooking spray.
  10. 10) Bake for 25 Minutes and remove the foil.
  11. 11) Bake for an additional 25 Minutes

    12. NOTE: Allow Lasagna to rest for 15 Minutes prior to serving
